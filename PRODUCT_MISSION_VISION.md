# ShredTrack — Mission, Vision & Product Description

> Reference document for product positioning, marketing copy, and brand voice across ShredTrack (the product) and ShredStack (the personal brand / portfolio site).

---

## Product Mission

> **ShredTrack helps hybrid athletes train smarter by turning every logged score into a clear, personalized signal — across CrossFit, HYROX, and recovery — so the work you put in actually compounds into the progress you want.**

**Why this works:** It names the user (hybrid athletes), the unique value (turning data into signal, not noise — the gap ShredTrack was built to fix), the scope (all three pillars), and the outcome (work that compounds). Specific enough to guide product decisions and broad enough to hold the roadmap.

### Shorter alternatives

- **"Make every rep count. ShredTrack turns your training data into the insights and plans that get you to your next PR — in CrossFit, HYROX, and everything in between."**
- **"We help athletes train with intent. ShredTrack is the one place to log, learn from, and level up every session — strength, conditioning, racing, and recovery."**

---

## Product Vision

> **A world where every athlete — not just the elite — trains with the clarity of a personal coach, the precision of a data scientist, and the support of a community. ShredTrack will be the operating system for the hybrid athlete: one home for CrossFit, HYROX, and recovery where your data writes your next plan, your gym's programming meets your personal goals, and the path from where you are to where you want to be is never a mystery.**

**Why this works:** A vision should describe a future state worth working toward for years. This one captures three things the product already points to:

1. Democratizing coach-level intelligence via AI
2. Unifying fragmented training silos (CrossFit, HYROX, recovery)
3. Bridging the individual ↔ gym relationship through communities, shared programming, and leaderboards

### Shorter alternative

> **"To be the operating system for the hybrid athlete — one place where CrossFit, HYROX, and recovery come together, and your training data writes your next plan."**

---

## Mission vs. Vision — the distinction

A quick gut check, because these two often get muddled:

- **Mission** = what you do every day, for whom, and why it matters *right now*.
- **Vision** = the future state your mission is in service of — the world you're trying to build.

You can think of the pair as: *"We do X (mission), because we believe in Y (vision)."*

## ShredStack Product Description

"ShredStack" is the personal brand; "ShredTrack" is the product. Below are three length variations depending on where each lives on the site.

### One-liner (hero / above the fold)

> **ShredTrack — the all-in-one training app for hybrid athletes. CrossFit, HYROX, and recovery, with AI-powered plans and the kind of data insights your gym app should have shipped years ago.**

### Short description (project card / portfolio tile, ~50 words)

> **ShredTrack** is a mobile-first training platform for CrossFit and HYROX athletes. It pairs everyday workout logging with AI-generated training plans, personalized performance insights, race prediction, and structured recovery — solving the fragmented, insight-poor experience that comes standard with most affiliate gym apps.

### Long description (about / project deep-dive, ~150 words)

> **ShredTrack** is a mobile-first training platform built for the modern hybrid athlete — the person who does CrossFit five days a week, races HYROX twice a year, and knows recovery isn't optional. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, gym whiteboards, and shallow tracker apps with one place to log every session and actually learn from it.
>
> Under the hood, ShredTrack turns daily score data into personalized insights: predicted 1RMs, RX-vs-scaled gap analysis, domain profiles (where you're strong, where you're soft), benchmark trends, and AI-extracted patterns from your own training notes. For HYROX athletes, it generates fully periodized training plans, predicts finish times against the global field using a model trained on real race data, and writes AI race reports after every practice race. Affiliate gyms get a community layer with shared programming, leaderboards, and recovery adherence — so coaches and athletes finally see the same picture.
>
> Built with Next.js, Drizzle, Supabase, and Claude. Designed for athletes who want their data to do more than sit in a database.

## Positioning Notes

Three things worth being deliberate about as copy gets written elsewhere:

1. **The "insights gap" is the sharpest wedge.** Most fitness apps log; very few interpret. ShredTrack has real interpretation logic — domain profiles, scaling-rate trends, notes extraction, ML race predictor. Lean on this language — *"insights, not just logs"* — anywhere ShredTrack's reason for existing needs explaining.

2. **"Hybrid athlete" is becoming a real category.** The CrossFit-meets-HYROX overlap is growing fast, and naming that audience explicitly will resonate more than generic "fitness tracking."

3. **Be careful with "all-in-one."** It's accurate but commodity-feeling. The stronger frame is *unified* — "one source of truth across CrossFit, HYROX, and recovery" — because it implies a thoughtful integration, not a kitchen sink.
